# Genre Knowledgebase

## Purpose

This directory stores genre specifications and reusable expression patterns for academic English writing.

Use a **genre specification** to decide the structure, stages, and organizing logic of a text.

Use an **expression pattern** file to choose reusable academic language after the genre has been selected.

## Genre Specifications

| Genre | Use When | Main Organizing Logic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Report Genre Specification](report.md) | The task describes stable knowledge about entities, classes, types, or parts. | Characteristics, classification, or composition |
| [Explanation Genre Specification](explanation.md) | The task explains how or why a phenomenon happens. | Causes, effects, events, or conditions |
| [Argument Genre Specification](argument.md) | The task defends a claim or weighs competing views on a debatable issue. | Claims, reasons, evidence, positions, and resolution |

## Quick Genre Selection

- Use **report** for `what it is`, `what types it has`, or `what parts it contains`.
- Use **explanation** for `how it happens`, `why it happens`, `what causes it`, or `what effects it produces`.
- Use **argument** for `what position should be accepted`, `which side is stronger`, or `why a claim is reasonable`.

## Common Confusions

### Report vs Explanation

A report organizes stable knowledge. An explanation organizes causal relations.

Example:

```text
Report: What are the types of renewable energy?
Explanation: Why does renewable energy reduce carbon emissions?
```

### Explanation vs Argument

An explanation shows causal logic. An argument uses reasons and evidence to support a debatable claim.

Example:

```text
Explanation: What effects may cyber bullying have on teenagers?
Argument: Should schools punish students for cyber bullying outside school?
```

### Report vs Argument

A report describes or classifies. An argument evaluates and defends a position.

Example:

```text
Report: What are the main types of driverless-car technology?
Argument: Should driverless cars replace traditional cars?
```
